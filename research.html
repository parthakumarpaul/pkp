Research Interests
Currently, I am working in theoretical High Energy Physics, specifically physics beyond the Standard Model, Dark Matter phenomenology, neutrino physics, and baryon asymmetry of the Universe. The only information about Dark Matter we know is the relic abundance of Dark Matter. Our main goal is to go beyond the SM of particle physics to build models incorporating Dark Matter candidates keeping all the constraints from direct, indirect, and collider searches satisfied. We are also trying to address the observed baryon asymmetry of the universe, neutrino mass, and Dark matter in a unified framework.
